What is Software Devlopment

Software development is the process of designing, creating, testing, and maintaining software applications and systems to meet specific user needs. It involves several stages, including requirements analysis, system design, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance. Each phase plays a crucial role in ensuring the final product is functional, reliable, and user-friendly.

Various methodologies, such as Agile, Waterfall, and DevOps, guide the software development process, influencing how teams collaborate and deliver projects. The ultimate goal is to produce high-quality software solutions that effectively address users' challenges while allowing for scalability and adaptability to changing requirements.
