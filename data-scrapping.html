Our FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Web scraping is legal when done in compliance with terms of service, data privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA), and without violating website restrictions. We ensure ethical and compliant data extraction.

We provide data in various formats such as CSV, JSON, Excel, XML, or direct database integration, depending on your business needs.

Web scraping should be done responsibly and in compliance with website terms of service, robots.txt, and data privacy laws. Some best practices include respecting website load times, using a delay between requests, handling CAPTCHAs, and avoiding scraping data that may be protected by copyright laws. Additionally, we recommend using anonymizing tools, such as VPNs or Tor, to protect your IP address and avoid being blocked by websites.

Data scraping can be challenging due to variations in website structures, dynamic content, and inconsistent data formats. To ensure data accuracy, we recommend using data validation techniques, such as checking for missing or incorrect values, comparing scraped data with known sources, and implementing error handling mechanisms.
