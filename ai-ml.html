Process

Process To Train & Develop AI

1.

Problem & Data Collection

We start by understanding your business needs and defining the problem AI/ML will solve. This includes gathering relevant structured and unstructured data from various sources.

2.

Data Preprocessing

Raw data is cleaned, transformed, and analyzed to remove inconsistencies. We then select the best machine learning algorithms based on the problem.

3.

Training & Optimization

The selected ML model is trained using large datasets, fine-tuned through hyperparameter adjustments, and validated for accuracy.

4.

Deployment & Improvement

Once trained and tested, the AI/ML model is deployed into your system via APIs, cloud platforms, or on-premise solutions.
